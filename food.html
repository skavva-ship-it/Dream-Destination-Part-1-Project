Restaurants in Dubai

Looking for a familiar buffet Restaurant? Look no further than the Kitchen6 restaurant with a window view like no other.

From home-style dishes like Pork and Eggs to Arab classics such as the famous yellow-lentil soup, there is always something for everyone here!

Finally, the restaurant also features a transparent kitchen so you are able to see food being made in front of your own eyes.
